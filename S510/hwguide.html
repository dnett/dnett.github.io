STAT 510 Homework Guidelines

Spring 2017

  • The purpose of all homework assignments in Statistics 510 is to help you learn the course material.
  • All homework assignments are posted at the Statistics 510 Homework Page.
  • Due dates are posted on the web site and written on the homework documents.
  • No late homework will be accepted, but your lowest homework score will not be used to compute your grade.
  • Answers to homework assignments should be neatly written or typed and well organized with no extraneous information.
  • You are welcome to work with other students on homework problems. You must, however, write your own answers to the questions. Copying another's work is prohibited.
